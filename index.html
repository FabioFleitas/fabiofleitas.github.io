PennCourseNotify

Website Link: www.penncoursenotify.com

Description: PennCourseNotify allows students at the University of Pennsylvania to be notified whenever a course has an opening via email. I used Python as the backend on Google App Engine to develop the site. It was featured on the front page of the Daily Pennsylvanian Newspaper and Website: http://www.thedp.com/r/375feaf5

Over 1,000 students are actively using the service.

Wesky Insurance Client SMS Notification System

Github Link: github.com/FabioFleitas/wesky-text

Description: Developed a client sms notification system for Wesky Insurance, a local insurance agency located in New Mexico (my hometown). Using Python, Google App Engine, and Twilio, the company is able to upload CSV files and send sms messages to clients reminding them of due payments. I also developed a way for the company to send custom messages to clients. With a built in login system, only employees are able to access the system.

Clients are very glad to be sent these sort of reminders and retention rates have significantly increased for the company because of this system.
