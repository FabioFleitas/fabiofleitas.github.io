Adding this extension to Chrome allows students at the University of Pennsylvania browse courses on PennInTouch and have PennCourseNotify "Notify Me!" buttons beside course listings. Wesky Insurance Client SMS Notification System

github repo

Developed a client sms notification system for Wesky Insurance, a local insurance agency located in New Mexico (my hometown). Using Python, Google App Engine, and Twilio, the company is able to upload CSV files and send sms messages to clients reminding them of due payments. I also developed a way for the company to send custom messages to clients. With a built in login system, only employees are able to access the system.

Clients are very glad to be sent these sort of reminders and retention rates have significantly increased for the company because of this system.
