PennCourseNotify

Website Link: www.penncoursenotify.com

Description: PennCourseNotify allows students at the University of Pennsylvania to be notified whenever a course has an opening via email. I used Python as the backend on Google App Engine to develop the site. It was featured on the front page of the Daily Pennsylvanian Newspaper and Website: http://www.thedp.com/r/375feaf5
Within 3 weeks, over 550 students have used the service.

PennGrub

Github Link: github.com/shynp/penngrub

Description: Built during PennApps Fall 2013. Coded in Python and for use with Google App Engine. PennGrub scraped data from the Penn dining halls and allocated them all in one mobile-friendly site. We also implemented a voting system that allowed student to vote on items they liked/disliked. It would then show the results from the previous time the item was featured in that dining hall.

I developed and programmed most of the backend for our app including the scraping algorithm, the database structure, and the voting system.
